I used Bootstrap 4 to create most of the style on this page, but I did some style myself :D . In Bootstrap you use class to style your page. When you want to change the background color of your element (an element is an HTML tag and all that is inside of it) you use 'bg'. You can set your background by placing a hyphen down dirrectly after bg and then typeing: primary, success, danger, warning, light, dark, secondary or info. If you want to change the color of your text you type 'text' instead of 'bg'.
